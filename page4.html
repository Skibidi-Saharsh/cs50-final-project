Fentanyl

Fentanyl is a man-made opioid that is prescribed by doctors as analgesic (treat pain) and an anesthetic. It is also commonly abused in the streets where it is also known as apache, dance fever, jackpot, murder 8, etc. It can be deadly in even small amounts.

Some of the effects of fentanyl are -

  • Drowsiness
  • Nausea or Vomiting

  • Urinary Retention

Some of the withdrawal symptoms of fentanyl are -
